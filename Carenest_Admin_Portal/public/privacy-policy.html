CareNest

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

CareNest ("we", "our", "us") is a caregiver booking platform designed for use in Sri Lanka. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and web services.

Last updated: March 17, 2026

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • User role (care receiver or caregiver)

Caregiver-Specific Information

  • National Identity Card (NIC) images for verification
  • Professional qualifications and certificates
  • Skills, experience, and service areas
  • Hourly rate and availability

Booking Information

  • Booking dates, times, and locations
  • Service type and care requirements
  • Booking status and history

Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Usage analytics (anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect care receivers with verified caregivers
  • Process and manage bookings
  • Verify caregiver identity and qualifications
  • Send booking notifications and updates
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database platform with enterprise-grade security. We implement the following measures:

  • All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Row-level security policies on all database tables
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Verification documents stored in secure cloud storage
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information in these cases:

  • Between users: Care receivers can see caregiver profiles (name, skills, rating). Caregivers can see booking details and patient name.
  • Verification: Admin staff review caregiver documents for identity verification purposes only.
  • Legal requirements: If required by Sri Lankan law or legal process.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Children's Privacy

CareNest is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us for immediate removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of CareNest after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
